Lowest: Application or procedure is working fine, however their could be improvement (feedback) Low: Application or personal procedure unusable, where a workaround is available or a repair is possible. Medium: Non-critical function or procedure, unusable or hard to use having an operational impact, but with no direct impact on services availability. High: Critical functionality or network access interrupted, degraded or unusable, having a severe impact on services availability. Highest: Interruption making a critical functionality inaccessible or a complete network interruption causing a severe impact on services availability.
